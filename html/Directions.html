Instructions on starting a sequence

The following instructions give a quick overview on how to use the sequce function within the sequence countdown page of the web application. These are steps that control the sequence countdowner, they inform the user on what steps to take to complete a sequence!

How to start a sequence in Sequence Master:

1) Select ‘Start a Sequence’ on the homepage, this will then take you to a new page called Sequence Countdown
2) Click on the blue ‘Settings’ Button, then a form will open, enter in the necessary details
3) Click on the blue ‘Start’ Button, the sequence will then commence
4) The red ‘Abandon’ Button can be selected at any time to stop the sequence
5) Once the sequence has finished the ‘OCS’ or ‘General’ Button can be selected depending on if any boats were over the start line

Throughout the sequence horns and audio warnings will be played automatically and the flags that need to go up or down will be displayed.
